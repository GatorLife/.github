# GATOR Mission & Vision Statements
### Mission:

**"To equip Cyber Warfare Operators with realistic, adaptive, AI-driven training environments and analytics that optimize readiness and operational excellence."**

### Vision:

**"To become the definitive standard in AI-powered cybersecurity training for the DoD, empowering every operator to anticipate, adapt, and excel against evolving cyber threats."**

### Proof of Concept:

“Enable a single Cyber Operator to complete a full Evaluation for the Senior Net Analyst role using static image environments.”

### Minimum Viable Product:

“Support multiple Cyber Operators in completing Evaluations for the Senior Net Analyst role, leveraging dynamically generated flags, AI-powered chatbot support, and automated results analysis.”
